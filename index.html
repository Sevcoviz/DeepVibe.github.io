The Spotify Manual

Follow these steps to export your raw data package.

STEP 01

Access Privacy Settings

Log into your account on spotify.com/account/privacy. Note: This cannot be done via the mobile or desktop app.

STEP 02

Request "Extended Streaming History"

Scroll down to "Download your data". Check the box for Extended streaming history. This provides the JSON files needed for our AI to be accurate.

STEP 03

Confirm & Wait

Spotify will send a confirmation email. Click the link inside. It takes 5-30 days for Spotify to prepare your package (they will email you once it's ready).

STEP 04

Extract and Upload

You will receive a .zip file. Drag it into DeepVibe!
